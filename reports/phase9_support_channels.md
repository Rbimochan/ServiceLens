# Phase 9 - Support Channel Analysis

Low CSAT is defined as scores 1–2. Response-time averages exclude the 3,128 invalid negative durations.

| Channel | Tickets | Average CSAT | Low CSAT % | High CSAT % | Average response minutes | Median response minutes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Email | 3,023 | 3.8991 | 23.16% | 73.47% | 216.95 | 7 |
| Inbound | 68,142 | 4.2514 | 14.32% | 82.70% | 175.06 | 6 |
| Outcall | 14,742 | 4.2699 | 13.95% | 83.18% | 172.34 | 6 |

Email is the weakest channel by average CSAT, low-CSAT rate, high-CSAT rate, and average valid response time. Outcall is the strongest by average CSAT and low-CSAT rate. These are descriptive associations, not causal effects.
