# Phase 9 - Key Pattern Discovery

## Channel Findings

- Email is the weakest channel: average CSAT 3.8991 and low-CSAT rate 23.16%.
- Outcall is strongest: average CSAT 4.2699 and low-CSAT rate 13.95%.

## Category Findings

- `Others` is lowest overall but has only 99 tickets.
- Cancellation is the weakest established category with 2,212 tickets, average CSAT 3.9905, and low-CSAT rate 21.47%.
- Returns is the largest category with 44,097 tickets and performs above the overall average.

## Sub-Category Findings

- With a minimum of 100 tickets, Technician Visit is weakest at 3.4894 average CSAT and 33.60% low CSAT.
- Seller Cancelled Order is second-worst at 3.5845 average CSAT and 30.41% low CSAT.

## Staffing Findings

- On Job Training is the weakest tenure bucket at 4.1452 average CSAT.
- Morning is the weakest shift at 4.1895 and has the largest shift volume.
- Agent-level values vary widely, but individual names are not ranked because volume and assignment mix strongly affect unadjusted rates.
- Supervisor and manager differences remain descriptive and unadjusted.

## Response-Time Findings

|---|---:|---:|---:|
| 0-5 minutes | 40,486 | 4.4844 | 8.88% |
| 6-15 minutes | 13,886 | 4.2578 | 13.88% |

| 31-60 minutes | 4,484 | 3.9605 | 21.07% |
| 61 minutes-24 hours | 15,182 | 3.8812 | 23.13% |
| More than 24 hours | 2,877 | 3.3952 | 36.32% |

CSAT declines sharply once valid response time exceeds 15 minutes. This is an unadjusted association and may be influenced by issue complexity, channel, workload, or other variables. The 3,128 negative durations are invalid and excluded from this pattern.
